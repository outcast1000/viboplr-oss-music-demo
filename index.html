▶ Add to Viboplr

Clicking the button opens Viboplr and subscribes to this catalog. Its tracks then appear in your library, search, and Home, and refresh automatically.

If the button does nothing (your Viboplr build may not handle music-source links yet), open Viboplr → Collections → + Add Music Source and paste this manifest URL:

https://raw.githubusercontent.com/outcast1000/viboplr-oss-music-demo/main/manifest.json
